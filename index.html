Blackjack Rules:

The basic rules of blackjack is simple. Players try to score as close to 21 as possible without exceeding 21. You are dealt two cards initially and if you aren't at 21 yet, you have the option to 'Hit', which means to draw another card.

If you ever exeed the 21, then you lose. If you do not wish to draw a card, then you may choose to 'Stand'. Whoever is then closer to 21, will win the game.

Might:

Might is the value of your money. The amount you bet will be doubled if you win, or lost when you lose. You may buy characters at the store to give you a bonus amount of might everytime you win.

Health:

If you reach 0 health, the dealer will level up, gaining more health. When you win a battle, you deal the amount of might you put in (including any bonuses), to the dealer. When you lose a battle, the dealer deals the amount of might you would have dealt, to you. If you defeat the dealer, you level up, gaining more base might and also more maximum health.

Allies:

Allies help you in different ways. Allies with a '+' icon, boost your might up by a set amount everytime you win. Allies with a 'x' icon, boost your might up by a multiplied amount everytime you win. Allies with a heart icon add a value to your maximum health and also heal you for that amount when you win.
